Buttermilk Pancakes

There's no better way to start your day!

There's something special about waking up to the smell of buttermilk pancakes. This dish is beginner-level, but it will please even the pickiest of eaters! Variations are simple to incorporate, just try adding some fruit or chocolate chips.

Warning: productivity and mobility after this dish not guaranteed.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsps white sugar
  • 3 tsps baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
