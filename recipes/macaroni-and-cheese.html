Macaroni and Cheese

Everyone loves macaroni and cheese!

What's better than ooey-gooey cheese covering a bowl of warm noodles? If you answered with "nothing", then you'd be correct!

This dish can be prepared by cooks of any skill level, and it's sure to leave anyone at the table smiling and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 tbsps all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups milk
  • 8 oz American cheese, cubed
  • 8 oz processed cheese food (e.g. Velveete), cubed
  • 1/4 cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
